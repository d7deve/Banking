About the projects in the depositary, basic steps on how to kick start a project.

Before touching code, answer in plain English:
What decision or question am I helping with?
Example: “Flag risky payments to review before settlement.”

Who would use this? (ops team, risk team, product, customer)

What would “good” look like?
Example: “Catch 90% of fraud with less than 5% false alarms.”
